LOBSTER ROLL (320 CALORIES), CRAB AND SHRIMP ROLLS (340 CALORIES)

We serve our lobster, crab and shrimp rolls Maine-style – a quarter pound of seafood served chilled atop a buttered, griddled New England split-top bun with a swipe of mayo, a dash of lemon butter and a sprinkle of our Secret Seasoning.

LOBSTER ROLL (320 CALORIES), CRAB AND SHRIMP ROLLS (340 CALORIES)

Try it all! Our Luke’s Trio offers half a lobster roll, half a crab roll, and half a shrimp roll. The perfect sampler for you to decide which is your favorite. Personalize your meal by adding sides, soups and more. Looking for a meal for two? We suggest two Luke’s Trios with your individual sides of choice.
